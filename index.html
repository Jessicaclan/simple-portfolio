My Background / About Me
I've always been fascinated by how people and technology meet. I hold a master's in Interaction Design from Aalborg University, where I spent five years exploring everything from tangible game controllers and gamified energy apps to AI-powered conversation tools and driving simulation interfaces. What sets me apart is that I don't just design, I build. With a solid front-end background in HTML, CSS, and Angular, I understand what it takes to bring an interface from concept to code. I've worked in-house at RadioAnalyzer, collaborating closely with colleagues to ship a real product, and I bring that same hands-on, iterative mindset to every project. I thrive when I'm solving real problems for real people, and I love the kind of work where curiosity leads the way.
